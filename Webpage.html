Alexander Fleming

Creator of Penicilin

Depiction of Alexander Fleming

Life Time Line

  • August 6, 1881
    Sir Alexander Fleming was born at Lochfield near Darvel in Ayrshire, Scotland.
  • 1908
    Gained his M.B.,B.S., with Gold Metal from St.Mary's;
  • 1928
    Found that mould culture prevented growth of staphylococci, even when diluted 800 times. He named the active substance penicillin.
  • 1945
    Received the Nobel Prize in Physiology or Medicine.
  • March 11, 1955
    Dr Fleming is burried in St.Paul's cathedral.
